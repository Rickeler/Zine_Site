Zines (derived from "Magazine") are self publications with a limited run, usually produced by one person or a small group. They were popularized in punk and DIY movements in the 80’s. Zines allow people who normally wouldn’t have a space in the traditional publishing world share their work, and often focus on under-represented topics. Anyone can make a zine and they can be about anything you want to share with the world.

1

Come up with your topic. This can be anything you’re passionate about. Create a dummy version of your zine to test the layout.

2

Generate the content. Write, collage, take photos, make a comic, illustrate. Your zine can be words, images or both.

3

Distribute your zine. Find a way to reproduce your zine, most zinesters photocopy. Then give it out or sell it!
